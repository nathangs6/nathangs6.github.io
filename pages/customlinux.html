Because Windows is trash, and Apple computers are expensive, I use Linux, Endeavour Linux. You can find a picture of my setup at the bottom of this page. Some basic details:

  • Terminal: kitty
  • Bar: polybar
  • Editor: vim
  • LaTeX Plugin: vimtex
  • PDF Viewer: zathura
